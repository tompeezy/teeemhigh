🃏 Wild Card Rules

Each player gets ONE wild card per round. Use it wisely.


Hit "Play Wild Card" to force any other player to drink. No reason required. No explanation owed. Just vibes.


Once it's gone, it's gone. Don't waste it on someone who's already drinking. Save it for when they're feeling smug about that par.

😂 Jim Rules

If someone named Jim is in your group (and there's always a Jim), enable Jim Tracker at setup.


Whenever Jim falls — in the cart, on the green, anywhere — tap "Jim Fell Down." All falls are logged.


At the end of the round, Jim's fall count is immortalized in the summary. As it should be.
